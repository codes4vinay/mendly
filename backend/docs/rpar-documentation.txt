RPAR - SERVICE PLATFORM DOCUMENTATION

================================
1. OVERVIEW
================================

RPAR is a comprehensive service booking and management platform that connects service providers with users who need services. The platform includes booking management, payments, real-time chat, notifications, and a review system.

Web URL: www.rpar.com
Customer Support Email: support@rpar.com

================================

4. SERVICE BOOKING GUIDE
================================

4.1 HOW TO BOOK A SERVICE
1. Navigate to Services
2. Browse or search for desired services
3. Select a service center location
4. Choose preferred date and time
5. Review service details and pricing
6. Proceed to checkout
7. Make payment via Razorpay
8. Receive booking confirmation

4.2 BOOKING STATUS
- PENDING: Awaiting service provider confirmation
- CONFIRMED: Service provider has accepted
- IN_PROGRESS: Service is being delivered
- COMPLETED: Service has been finished
- CANCELLED: Booking was cancelled

4.3 CANCELLATION & RESCHEDULING
- Cancel up to 24 hours before booking
- Rescheduling available based on availability
- Refund policies apply per service terms
- Contact support for special requests

10. TECHNICAL SUPPORT
================================

10.1 COMMON ISSUES
- Login problems: Verify email/password, use forgot password
- Payment issues: Check card details, try different payment method
- Booking errors: Clear cache, try different browser
- Chat not loading: Refresh page, check internet connection

10.2 TROUBLESHOOTING
- Update mobile app to latest version
- Clear browser cookies and cache
- Disable ad blockers
- Try incognito/private browsing mode

10.3 REPORT BUGS
- Email with detailed description to support@rpar.com
- Include screenshots and browser information
- Provide reproduction steps
- Bug bounty program available

12. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================

12.1 BOOKING QUESTIONS
Q: How long does it take to confirm a booking?
A: Usually 2-4 hours, confirmed bookings are updated in real-time

Q: Can I book for someone else?
A: Yes, provide their details during booking

Q: What if the service provider doesn't show up?
A: Contact support immediately for refund and rebooking options

12.2 PAYMENT QUESTIONS
Q: Is my payment information safe?
A: Yes, we use Razorpay with end-to-end encryption

Q: Can I pay in installments?
A: Some services may offer installment options, check at checkout

Q: What if payment fails?
A: Retry with same or different payment method; contact support if issue persists

12.3 GENERAL QUESTIONS
Q: Is there an age requirement?
A: Must be 18+ to use the platform

Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account (data deleted after 30 days)

Q: Is RPAR available on mobile?
A: Yes, available on iOS and Android via app stores
